About Us

EUROEXIM BUSINESS MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCY SERVICES INC. (EEBMCSI) headquartered at #18 Leo Alejandrino St., BF Resort Village, Barangay Talon Dos, Las Piñas City, Philippines, was established in 2020 as a strategic subsidiary of EURO EXIM CONSULTANCY LTD., a key entity within the TOPWAY FAREAST LTD. group. EEBMCSI is a premier provider of business consultancy services. The company is committed to driving business success through strategic solutions in digital transformation, financial services, business process optimization, and international trade consultancy. Under the leadership of Chairman Jesus B. Varela, President Angelo G. Santiago, and Operating Director Lyzette Abaya-Mede, EEBMCSI has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses across multiple industries. The company takes pride in delivering world-class expertise and high-value solutions that enhance operational efficiency, foster growth, and enable companies to navigate today’s complex global marketplace. Additionally, EEBMCSI is the official and exclusive distributor of EdgeTI and Parasoft solutions in the Philippines, further solidifying its role as a leader in technology-driven business consultancy.

EdgeTI: Revolutionizing Business Operations with Intelligent Data Visualization

Edge Technologies, Inc. (EdgeTI) is a leading provider of real-time operational intelligence, data visualization, and system integration solutions. The company specializes in helping organizations enhance decision-making by providing a unified interface for managing complex IT ecosystems. With over two decades of experience, EdgeTI has established itself as a trusted partner for enterprises, government agencies, and multinational corporations worldwide.

Key Solutions & Capabilities

Edge Total Intelligence (EdgeTI)

• EdgeTI is an advanced operational intelligence platform that integrates disparate data sources, IT systems, and operational tools into a single, unified command and control interface.

• The platform provides real-time data visualization, AI-powered insights, and predictive analytics, enabling businesses to improve situational awareness and decision-making.

• EdgeTI is designed to eliminate data silos, allowing for seamless communication and coordination across different departments and technologies.

Use Cases & Industry Applications

• Government & Defense – Enhances security operations, cyber intelligence, and national defense strategies through real-time monitoring.

• Financial Services – Improves fraud detection, regulatory compliance, and operational risk management.

• Healthcare – Streamlines hospital operations, patient data analysis, and emergency response systems.

• Telecommunications & Smart Cities – Facilitates infrastructure monitoring, resource management, and IoT integration.

• Supply Chain & Logistics – Optimizes inventory tracking, fleet management, and logistics operations.

Core Features

• Unified Operational Intelligence – Combines multiple IT and business applications into a single dashboard, allowing decision-makers to see the full operational landscape.

• AI & Machine Learning Capabilities – Uses predictive analytics to identify trends, detect anomalies, and provide intelligent recommendations for operational efficiency.

• Cybersecurity & Compliance – Ensures secure access control, real-time threat detection, and regulatory compliance (GDPR, ISO, NIST).

• Scalability & Customization – Adapts to businesses of any size and integrates with existing enterprise systems (ERP, CRM, IoT, and more).

Competitive Advantage

• Unlike traditional Business Intelligence (BI) tools, EdgeTI offers real-time, interactive dashboards that aggregate and normalize data from multiple sources—reducing information overload and response time.

• The company’s solutions are trusted by global enterprises, including government agencies, large financial institutions, and Fortune 500 companies

Why Businesses Choose EdgeTI:

EdgeTI is the preferred choice for enterprises looking to accelerate digital transformation and gain real-time control over their operations. By implementing EdgeTI, companies experience reduced downtime, enhanced decision-making, and significant cost savings. EEBMCSI, as the exclusive distributor, provides end-to-end consultation, implementation, and technical support to ensure seamless integration of EdgeTI into any business environment.

Parasoft: Ensuring Software Quality, Security, and Compliance

Parasoft is a global leader in automated software testing and software quality assurance solutions. The company helps organizations develop high-quality, secure, and compliant applications by automating software testing throughout the software development lifecycle (SDLC). Parasoft's AI-powered testing solutions are widely used by enterprises in finance, healthcare, automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, and government sectors.

Key Solutions & Capabilities

Comprehensive Automated Testing Solutions

• Static Code Analysis – Identifies security vulnerabilities, coding errors, and performance issues at an early stage.

• Unit Testing & API Testing – Automates functional and regression testing to improve software reliability.

• Service Virtualization – Simulates complex, dependent systems to accelerate testing without delays.

• Performance & Load Testing – Ensures applications can handle real-world usage under high-traffic conditions.

• Continuous Testing for DevOps – Enables integration with CI/CD pipelines for faster software deployment.

Industry Applications & Compliance

• Finance & Banking – Ensures secure transactions and helps meet regulatory requirements such as PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, and GDPR.

• Healthcare & Medical Devices – Supports compliance with FDA, HIPAA, and ISO 13485 for software used in healthcare applications.

• Automotive & Aerospace – Ensures compliance with safety-critical standards such as ISO 26262 (Automotive) and DO-178C (Aerospace & Defense).

• Government & Defense – Provides secure software development solutions for mission-critical applications.

Core Features

• AI-Driven Test Automation – Uses machine learning to optimize test cases, detect anomalies, and prioritize high-risk areas.

• Security Testing & Vulnerability Detection – Identifies weaknesses in web, mobile, and cloud-based applications to prevent cyber threats.

• Continuous Integration & DevOps Support – Integrates seamlessly with tools like Jenkins, GitHub, Docker, Kubernetes, and Azure DevOps.

• Regulatory Compliance & Risk Management – Ensures that applications meet international security and compliance standards.

Competitive Advantage

• Unlike traditional manual testing, Parasoft's AI-driven solutions automate testing at every stage of development, reducing time-to-market and minimizing defects.

• Parasoft helps businesses cut costs by identifying issues early, eliminating rework, and improving overall software quality.

Why Businesses Choose Parasoft:

With increasing cybersecurity threats and the need for software reliability, Parasoft helps businesses minimize risks, improve compliance, and accelerate development timelines. Organizations using Parasoft benefit from cost-effective testing automation, enhanced security, and faster deployment. As the exclusive distributor, EEBMCSI provides expert consultation, implementation services, and ongoing support to ensure businesses maximize the value of Parasoft’s solutions.
